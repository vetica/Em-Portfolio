I'm Em Poertner.
Writer, editor, designer, and
overall badass from Roanoke, Virginia.

I’ve always struggled to write ‘about’ sections for myself because I fluctuate between being a closed book and an oversharer. Plus I’m never quite sure what people actually want to glean from these things.

I live and work in Roanoke, Virginia - a place I picked because of childhood-ties and because it’s absolutely beautiful 365 days a year (also there’s a ton of great craft beer.)

I started my career working for the Louisville Design Center. In my time there I designed print newspaper sections for sites as small as the Fremont (Ohio) News-Messenger to as large as the Detroit Free Press. It was in Louisville that I ran my favorite 5K. It was the Hungry Turkey Run - a Thanksgiving 5K where at the end runners were given giant cinnamon rolls (I believe they now give out giant donuts, which is equally as amazing.)

Since January 2022, I’ve worked for the USA Today Network - Tennessee as a sports planning editor and project specialist. I do a little bit of everything including, but not limited to, managing project timelines, planning sports sections, writing newsletters and designing visuals.

I treasure my free time dearly because I have too much I like to do in my time off. I play Ultimate, run, hike, read, write, draw, garden, craft, do DIYs, and watch football in the fall (Packers fan - no, I’m not upset about him going to the Jets.)

Resume
USA Today Network - Tennessee, Remote - Sports Planning Editor & Project Specialist
JAN 2022 - PRESENT

At the core of my job animated, I collaborate with editors, reporters and the production team to build content plans for USA Today Network - Tennessee publications including the Nashville Tennessean and the Knoxville News Sentinel. I use audience analytics from Pars.ly and Google Analytics to inform our digital strategy and grow subscriptions. I also curate, edit and help write newsletters for different target audiences including Nashville’s Daily Briefing.

For our enterprise work, I manage the project’s timeline and develop compelling visuals for on-platform and off-platform promotion.

USA Today Network Design Center, Remote — Team Leader
APRIL 2021 - JAN 2022

As a team leader, I assisted in managing the production of daily print sports sections for the USA Today Network's Midwest and Ohio regions. I mentored designers and assisted with training, scheduling staff, and employee relations. I also designed and page edited daily print sports sections and special projects.

During my time with the studio, including my time as a designer, I was part of the Diversity Steering Committee. As part of the group, I helped influence department policies around how we represent diverse communities in the newspaper. I designed, edited and helped write the intradepartmental newsletter our team created.

Louisville Design Studio/USA Today Network Design Center, Louisville, KY — Designer & Page Editor
SEPT 2016 - APRIL 2021

I was hired by the Louisville Design Studio fresh out of college. I designed and page edited daily news and sports sections for USA Today Network publications including the Louisville Courier Journal and Cincinnati Enquirer. Football season was always our busiest time. I worked on a variety of special sections including the 2020 Alabama Gameday wraps (for the Tuscaloosa News), the 2019 Clemson Gameday section (for the Greenville News) and high school football preview sections galore.

Creasey Mahan Nature Preserve, Goshen, KY — Freelancer
MARCH 2019 - APRIL 2022

I started volunteering at Creasey Mahan in their woodland gardens in 2018, and was brought on as a freelancer in 2019 to help with the Butterfly Ball, an annual fundraising gala. I designed flyers promoting the event, the program, and a plethora of ancillary posters and printed material for the event from 2019-2022.
